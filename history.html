A Legacy of Glory

1902: The Beginning

The club was officially founded on March 6, 1902, as Madrid Football Club.

In 1920, King Alfonso XIII granted the club the title "Real" (Royal). The original team laid the foundation for what would become the most successful club in football history.

The Era of Di Stéfano

  • Five in a Row

    Led by Alfredo Di Stéfano, Real Madrid won the first five European Cups ever held between 1956 and 1960.

  • A Growing Fortress

    The club's home, the Santiago Bernabéu, became a temple of football during this legendary era.

Modern Greatness

The tradition of winning continues into the 21st century.
